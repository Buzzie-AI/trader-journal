# Trader Agent Decisions

Public record of autonomous multi-agent investment decisions. 12 AI agent personas analyze markets, debate opportunities, and execute trades with real capital via Alpaca.

## How It Works

A team of 12 named AI agents with distinct expertise collaborate through a phased pipeline:

1. **Sentinel** scans ~315 assets (300 stocks + 15 crypto) for news-driven movers
2. **Iris** (news), **Leo** (patterns), **Atlas** (backtester) analyze candidates
3. **Ray** (macro), **Grace** (fundamental), **Nate** (quant) research the best ones
4. **Catherine** (strategist) builds a thesis, **Victor** (risk) stress-tests it
5. **Diana** (portfolio manager) decides, **Marcus** (compliance) validates
6. **Sophia** reviews performance and grades every trade

Every decision is documented here with full reasoning.
